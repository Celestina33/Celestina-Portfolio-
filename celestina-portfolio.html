Hi Sarah,

Thank you for flagging this — I completely understand how frustrating an unexpected charge can be, and I want to sort this out for you right away.

I've reviewed your account and can confirm that a duplicate transaction did occur on 14th June. I've raised a refund of $34.99 which will appear in your account within 3–5 business days.

I've also added a note to your file to ensure this doesn't happen again. Please don't hesitate to reach back out — you have my direct contact above.

Warm regards,

Live Chat · Technical Issue

Customer: "Your app keeps crashing and I can't log in."

Hi! I'm really sorry to hear that — that must be incredibly frustrating, especially if you're trying to access something important. Let me help you right now. 😊

Could you try clearing your app cache first? On most phones: Settings → Apps → [App name] → Clear Cache. Then reopen the app and try logging in again.

If that doesn't work, I can escalate this to our technical team with high priority and get you a temporary access link while it's being fixed. Which would you prefer?

Email · Cancellation Retention

Customer: "I'd like to cancel my subscription."

Hi Marcus,

I'm sorry to hear you'd like to cancel — I want to make sure we've done everything we can to make this work for you first.

Could I ask what's been the main reason? If it's the cost, I can offer you 30% off your next 3 months. If it's a feature you feel is missing, I'd love to share what's coming in our next update.

Of course, if you'd still like to proceed, I'll process that immediately with no fuss. Either way, I appreciate your time with us. 🙏
